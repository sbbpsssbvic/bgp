About

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Beni Gaddau Pur village is 166862. Beni Gaddau Pur village is located in Bikapur tehsil of Faizabad district in Uttar Pradesh, India. It is situated 11km away from sub-district headquarter Bika Pur (tehsildar office) and 31km away from district headquarter Faizabad. As per 2009 stats, Beni Gaddopur is the gram panchayat of Beni Gaddau Pur village.
